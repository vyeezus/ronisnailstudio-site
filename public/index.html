Please read our policy below before you book.

Deposit Policy

  • A non-refundable $50 deposit (Zelle: ronisnailstudio@gmail.com) is required for first-time clients and goes towards your service total.
  • Deposits are transferable if you reschedule or cancel at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Cancellations or rescheduling within 24 hours will forfeit the deposit.
  • Limit of 2 reschedules per booking. After that, a new deposit will be required.

Cancellation & Lateness

  • There is a 10 minute grace period. Over 10 minutes, a $15 late fee applies if I am still able to see you.
  • Over 15 minutes late, your appointment may be automatically cancelled.
  • If no deposit was made, a $50 rebooking fee will apply.

No Shows

  • Deposit will be forfeited immediately.
  • To rebook, you must prepay the full service amount (non-refundable).
  • Clients who no-show more than once will be permanently banned from booking.

Service Info

  • To inquire about weekend availability, please contact me directly—those times are not shown on the public calendar.
  • Appointments typically last 1 to 2.5 hours. Intricate designs may take 3+ hours.
  • Pricing is determined by the level of difficulty and time required.
  • Refills are recommended every 3 weeks. Anything 4+ weeks is charged as a new set.
  • Please send inspo pics 2-3 days before your appointment. Exact replicas are not always possible. No freestyles.

Studio Standards

  • Cash or Zelle only. $5 discount when paying cash. Tips are Venmo or Cash only.
  • Private 1:1 studio. No guests or children unless arranged prior.
  • I do not work over other artists' work.
  • I do not work on nails with severe damage or open wounds.

Refunds

  • No Refunds. Please make sure you are satisfied during or immediately after your appointment.
